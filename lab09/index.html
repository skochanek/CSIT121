French Toast Casserole

Out of this world Frenct Toast Casserole

  • 1 loaf French Bread, cut in 1 inch slices
  • 8 eggs
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 3/4 cup butter
  • 1 1/3 cups brown sugar
  • 3 tablespoons light corn syrup
  1. Butter a 9x3 inch baking dish. Arrange the slices of bread in the bottom.
  2. In a large bowl, beat together eggs, milk, cream, vanilla and cinnamon. Pour over bread slices, cover, and refrigerate overnight.
  3. The next morning, preheat oven to 350 degress F.
  4. In a small saucepan, combine butter, brown sugar and corn syrup; heat until bubbling. Pour over bread and egg mixture.
  5. Bake uncovered, for 40 minutes on middle rack.
  6. Place a pan or foil on lower rack to catch any spillover, do not place pan or foil on bottom of oven.

I have always loved French Toast. This takes it to the next level, the sauce that you pour over is what does it. It is sweet and a little savory, it is the perfect combination!
